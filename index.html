EcoBlock

HooHacks 2021

Project Goal: During a hackathon (HooHacks 2021), me and my friends decided to take a step toward environmental change by created a form of cryptocurrency which differentiates itself from others by using less energy. We've looked at the current trend of crypto such as Bitcoin and many more which relies on many computers computing hashes on a blockchain which solely relies on the pure computer power generating tons of waste energy. Our team came up with a solution to solve this by creating a cryptocurrency that relies on one's environmental footprint and luck.

Project Idea: Our project, EcoBlock uses a Proof of Stake system which is a "lottery" type system to distribute its currency. This ideology is different from Bitcoin because Bitcoin relies on a Proof of Work system which relies on the computing power of a computer, thus creating waste energy. Therefore, we've created a way to distribute our currency to others by a "lottery" system which also factors one's environmental impacts as a way to increase their odds.

My Task: I worked as full-stack creating endpoints for EcoBlock. I consolidated the Front-end Flutter application with the Backend blockchain using Firebase BaaS.

Award: 1st Place for Best use of Google Cloud

nwHacks 2021

Certi-Chain

Hack Western 7

Project Goal: During a hackathon (Hack Western 7), my team and I decided to help the economy by creating a solution which help authenticate product and reduce the number of fake goods in the world.

Project Idea: Certi-Chain uses a python based blockchain to authenticate any products with a Certi-Chain NFC tag. Each tag will contain a unique ID attached to the blockchain that cannot be faked. Users are able to tap their phones on any product containing a Certi-Chain tag to view the authenticity of a product through the Certi-Chain blockchain. Additionally if the product is authentic users are also able to see where the products materials were sourced and assembled.

My Task: My role as a full-stack developer is to implement the HTML and CSS to work as templates for the flask application through jinja2. I also made the app work through a URL query parameters enabling NFC capabilities.

Award: Best Hardware Hack Sponsored by Digi-Key
